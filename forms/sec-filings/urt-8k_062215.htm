Item 5.07 Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.

 

On June 17, 2015, United Realty Trust Incorporated (the “Company”) held its annual meeting of stockholders. The matters submitted to the stockholders for a vote included (1) the election of three directors to serve until the 2016 annual meeting of stockholders and until their successors are duly elected and qualified, and (2) the ratification of the appointment of Ernst & Young LLP as the Company’s independent auditor for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2015.
